Small Groups

Small Groups meet throughout the week in homes across the Sugar Land area. They're one of the best ways to build real friendships and grow in faith together.

Find a group that fits your life

Groups are organized by neighborhood, life stage, and interest. Browse the current list on our website, or fill out the signup form and we'll help you find a fit.

New to Small Groups? Sign up and a group leader will reach out within a week.
